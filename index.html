you're connected to every other human in the world right now

but doesn't it feel like more should be happening?

the internet is a vast creative canvas

anyone can contribute to it

you can draw, write, or show just about anything

why isn't it more alive?

is it because:

  • platforms lock us in and steal our attention?
  • the media tires us out with panic inducing headlines?
  • there's just too many possibilities and choices to sift through?

when was the last time you put something out there for the world to see?

the reason the world feels like it's not as alive is because you're not contributing to it and remixing it

i'm making a static site host / social network / website builder / open-source platform where everything is remixable by default

  • defend your site against a hoard of tiny alients that want to eat all your written text -- mmm text yummy!
  • plant a garden around your favorite poem and watch vines from the garden intertwine with the poem's words
  • send your friend a set of monarch butterflies that fly all over their site and redesign it with colors you choose

it's time for a wilder web

will you join me?
